LaBoutik - Cash register / Cashless : https://laboutik.demo.tibillet.org/​

To see the cash register interface, click on “view site” on the administration page.

I scan the QrCode on my piggy bank card: https://lespass.demo.tibillet.org/qr/c2b2400c-1f7e-4305-b75e-8c1db3f8d113/​

In the LaBoutik interface, this card corresponds to “Customer 1”.

The page we arrive at after a qrcode allows you to top up your piggy bank, view your memberships and declare your lost card:

Fedow - Federation creation: https://fedow.demo.tibillet.org/dashboard/​

A piggy bank card, a coin and a membership can be used in one or more locations. This is referred to as a federation of locations.
